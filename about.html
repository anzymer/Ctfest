About the Department

The Department of Computer Technology was established in the year 2015 with the objective of imparting Quality Education in the field of Computing Technologies. We have made tremendous progress and have become one of the most vibrant departments within the College. The Department offers a 3-year Bachelor of Computer Technology (B.Sc-Computer Technology) programme from the year 2015.

The guidance and cooperation of the experienced and expert faculty members of the department is one of the attracting features of the department, which enlivens the students to stretch their wings, so that they can fly higher than their dreams and serve the country. The Department has state of the art infrastructure in terms ICT equipped class rooms and well equipped separate computing labs for students.

In consequence to their arduous training and exposure with the state of the arts professional programs offered, the students graduating from this department get placed in the leading IT industries even before the completion of their final semesters. There is plethora of companies, like Zoho International, TCS, CTS, Deloitte, Accenture, Infosys, HCL Info system, Wipro, Calibrant Technologies etc, that recruits the passing out students each year through campus placement. The students have provision of 2 years of rigorous training for the placement in reputed organizations.
